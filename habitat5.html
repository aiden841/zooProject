The arctic is surrounded by water. But it is frozen. The arctic is a very cold place. Its full of glaciers, iceberbs and very cold water.

Walrus

They are usually brown or pink. Their fur covers all of their bodies except for fins. They have two small eyes, a mustache and two long tusks. They also have longer and thicker tusks, and usually have thicker skin.

Polar Bear

Think of a polar bear as a regular bear but white and it lives in the cold arctic. Their bodies are slim but they have a long neck. They have webbed toes and claws. The polar bear's body is full of hair except their nose, lips and claws.
