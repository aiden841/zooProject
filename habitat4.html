The marine water has very high salt content. It usually has rocks and corals where alot of animals live.

Crab

Crabs are crustaceans with a short tail and are covered with a thick shell with a pair of claws. ... Crabs have no backbone. Their shell is to protects them from predators and provides support for their bodies.

Octopus

Octopus is a eight legged sea animal. they are similar to squids. They also dont have a skeleton. Under their bodies, it is full of suction cups that help them move and take grip into something.
